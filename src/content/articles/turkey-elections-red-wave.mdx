# Turkey Elections: Red Wave Conquers Anatolia

The 2024 local elections in Turkey marked a historic shift in the political landscape, with the main opposition party CHP making unprecedented gains.

## Key Findings

- CHP won in 35 provinces, up from 21 in 2019
- Major cities including Istanbul, Ankara, and Izmir remained in opposition hands
- Ruling AKP experienced significant losses in traditionally conservative areas

## Geographic Analysis

The data reveals a clear pattern of opposition expansion from coastal regions into the Anatolian heartland.

## Methodology

This analysis uses official election results from the Supreme Election Council (YSK), combined with demographic data to identify trends and patterns.
